Our Story

Founded in 2010, Glasser UAE has grown from a small glass installation company to one of the leading providers of premium glass solutions in the United Arab Emirates.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to transform spaces with innovative glass solutions while maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety.
